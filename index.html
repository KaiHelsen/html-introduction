A funny story

Several years ago, me and some friends from College were crunching on our 1st year project. For this project, we worked on a small house in maya

As everyone was stressed and under pressure, people went a little loopy. Up until the point one of my friends asked: Does this circle look round?

Upon hearing this strange question I burst out in laughter and simply couldn't stop. Several others caught on and started laughing as well

For about ten minutes, we just laughed while trying to work before we managed to regain a semblance of sanity.

However, it started back up the moment someone said "Have you seen the book?"

TL;DR

Someone saying Does this circle look round is very funny when you're stressed and tired.
